# ST125 Six Phases – Extended Definitions (Weekly Chart)

## Phase X – Correction Phase (Bottoming / Start of Uptrend)

- X1: MA52 > MA26 > MA10, all slopes negative, MA2 crosses above MA10  
  → Early reversal signal after downtrend, minor bounce.

- X2: MA52 > MA26 > MA10, slopes of MA52 & MA26 negative, MA2 crosses above MA10, SAR low dots  
  → Confirmed early bullish divergence, potential bottom.

- X3: MA52 > MA26, MA10 between MA26 & MA52, slopes of MA52/26 negative, MA2 > MA10  
  → Gradual buying absorption, still in correction.

- X4: MA52 > MA10 > MA26, slope of MA52 negative, MA2 slope turns positive  
  → Weak uptrend beginning, still below year line.

- X5: MA10 > MA52 > MA26, slope of MA52 negative → MA10 pulls above 52 but long trend still down → fakeout possible.

## Phase B – Overheating Phase (Bull Peak / Exhaustion)

- B1: MA10 > MA26 > MA52, all slopes positive  
  → Strong bull trend, euphoria zone.

- B2: MA10 > MA26 > MA52, all slopes positive, current slope of MA10 > previous slope of MA10
